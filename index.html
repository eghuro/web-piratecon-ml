Successful elections

The local elections in October 2014 were a huge success for the Czech Pirate party. X Pirates and many independent candidates under the Pirate flag were elected to local city councils.

The biggest vistory was Marienbad. With 21% there is a local mayor and 2 other Pirates in the board. The coalition has decided on 6 priorities for the upcoming term: Transparent and responsible governance of the city, Participation of citizens on decision making, Quality public services, Urban development, Prevention of youth drain, Spa and tourism expansion.
